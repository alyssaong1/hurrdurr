WHAT IS IT?
The MSA programme focuses on 3 key initiatives to launch students into a career in IT.

Inspire

Inspiring High School Students into a career in IT through hands-on app development workshops using TouchDevelop.

Develop

Preparing Tertiary Students for work through voluntary work placement over the summer break working on innovative projects.

Innovate

Students work in teams and develop world-changing ideas through our startup competition, the Microsoft Imagine Cup.

Program Schedule
Show all the phases of MSA here.

  • March-September 2015

    Phase 1

    Students must complete a set of training requirements to get accepted into the programme.

  • September-October 2015

    Company Registrations

    Companies interested in taking on student teams over the summer must register for the programme by October 24th.

  • November 2015

    Phase 2

    Intense set of training for students based on Apps and Azure with assessments to qualify for placements.

    Companies will complete a form that will provide insight into the type of project, type of students sought after and information about the company.

  • December-February 2015

    Placement with Employers

    Placement term with companies over the summer.

  • February 2015

    Phase 3

    MSA High School Initiative in place over the month of February.

  • March 2016

    Phase 4

    At the completion of the programme, we invite all teams and employers to join us at the Microsoft Student Accelerator Showcase where teams will have an opportunity to showcase their summer projects.
